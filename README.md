## About

I'm an AI Engineer focused on **agentic AI systems** — LLM applications that reason, retrieve, and act, not just generate text. My work centers on Retrieval-Augmented Generation, multi-agent orchestration, and turning LLM APIs into reliable, structured applications.

I care about the parts of AI engineering that don't show up in demos: retrieval quality, evaluation, structured outputs, and systems that behave predictably in production. I build with Python and FastAPI on the backend, LangChain and CrewAI for orchestration, and vector databases (ChromaDB, FAISS) for retrieval.

Currently completing an applied AI Engineering track at **Xeven Solutions**, shipping daily and documenting the work publicly.

## Featured AI Projects

### Heart Disease Prediction — Clinical Risk Classifier
**Problem:** Predicting heart disease risk from patient clinical data to support early screening decisions.
**Solution:** A binary classification pipeline benchmarking multiple models on the UCI Heart Disease dataset, with feature engineering and ROC-AUC-based evaluation to select the strongest performer.
**Tech Stack:** `Python` `Scikit-learn` `Pandas` `NumPy` `Jupyter`
**Key Features:**
- Comparative evaluation across Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and SVM
- Feature engineering pipeline for clinical tabular data
- Model selection driven by ROC-AUC, not just accuracy

[View Repository →](https://github.com/seharandleeb/heart-disease-prediction)

**Problem:** Most ML learning stays in notebooks and never reaches applied, reviewable engineering practice.
