## 🔮 About Me

I'm an **AI Engineer** focused on agentic AI systems — LLM applications that reason, retrieve, and act, not just generate text. My work centers on **Retrieval-Augmented Generation**, **multi-agent orchestration**, and turning LLM APIs into structured, reliable applications.

I care about the parts of AI engineering that don't show up in demos: retrieval quality, evaluation, structured outputs, and systems that behave predictably in production. I build with **Python** and **FastAPI** on the backend, **LangChain** and **CrewAI** for orchestration, and vector databases like **ChromaDB** and **FAISS** for retrieval.

Currently completing an applied AI Engineering track at **Xeven Solutions**, shipping daily and documenting the work in public.
